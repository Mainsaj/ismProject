Freeskate

Designed to teach advanced figure skating techniques to skaters who completed the Basic Skills program

  1. Forward inside open mohawk from a standstill position (R to L and L to R)
  2. Backward crossovers to a backward outside edge glides (landing position), clockwise and counterclockwise
  3. Backward outside edge to a forward outside edge transition, clockwise and counterclockwise
  4. Two forward crossovers into a forward inside mohawk, step down and cross behind, step into one backward crossover and step to a forward inside edge, clockwise and counterclockwise
  5. One-foot upright spin, optional entry and free-foot position (minimum three revolutions)
  6. Mazurka (R and L)
  7. Waltz jump
  8. Backward inside pivots
  1. Forward power stroking, clockwise and counterclockwise
  2. Basic forward outside and forward inside consecutive edges (four to six consecutive edges)
  3. Backward outside three-turns (R and L)
  4. Upright spin, entry from back crossovers (min. four to six revolutions)
  5. Half flip
  6. Toe loop
  7. Waltz jump-side toe hop-waltz jump sequence, or waltz jump-ballet jump-toe loop sequence
  1. Alternating forward outside and inside spirals on a continuous axis (two sets)
  2. Basic backward outside and backward inside consecutive edges (four to six consecutive edges)
  3. Backward inside three-turns (R and L)
  4. Beginning back spin (up to two revolutions)
  5. Half Lutz
  6. Salchow
  7. Variation of a forward spiral, skater’s choice
  1. Alternating backward crossovers to back outside edges – Four sets
  2. Alternating mohawk/crossover sequence (R to L and L to R)
  3. Waltz three-turns (forward outside three turn, backward outside edge glide), clockwise and counterclockwise
  4. Advanced back spin with free foot in crossed leg position (min. three revolutions)
  5. Loop jump
  6. Waltz jump-toe loop or Salchow-toe loop combination
  7. Toe step sequence, skater’s choice, using a variety of toe steps
  1. Forward power three-turns (R and L), 3 sets each

  3. Forward upright spin to backward upright spin (3 revs., each foot)
  4. Sit spin (minimum three revolutions)
  5. Half loop

  7. Split jump, stag jump or split falling leaf

  2. Five-step mohawk sequence, clockwise and counterclockwise

  6. Loop-loop combination
